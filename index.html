QR Copy Shortcut

A simple Chrome extension to generate QR codes instantly.

Features

  • Generate QR codes for the current webpage or selected text.
  • Copy QR codes directly to your clipboard.
  • Download QR codes as images.
  • Supports links, phone numbers, emails, and WhatsApp formats.
  • One-click or Alt+Q shortcut to open the generator.

Privacy

QR Copy Shortcut works entirely in your browser. It does not collect or transmit personal data. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
